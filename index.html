I am a musician, educator, director and software developer. My experiences in music have allowed me to creatively problem solve as well as assist fellow musicians in understanding their strengths and improving their weak spots. These experiences have been preparing me for a career as a programmer, where the ability to identify a problem and find creative solutions in a collaborative environment is central to the way they work.
×

Most recently I served as the Director of Music Ministries at the United Methodist Church in Stow and Adjunct Instructor of Bassoon at the College of Wooster. I also teach bassoon privately, substitute in the Stow schools and am the Principal Bassoonist in the Lakeside Symphony Orchestra. I have touched upon programming at several points my career, the very first time being during my course work for a MA in Music Education. I used ASCII to manipulate sound envelopes and then use them in an arrangement of a short piece of music. My interest was further piqued in classes I took renew my teaching licensure. I love the challenges and creative potential in coding. I have a wonderful husband, two great kids and a spastic dog.
