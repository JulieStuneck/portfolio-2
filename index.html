Front-end or back-end, I love the art in code. I appreciate the beauty of logically laid out html and the elegance of a concise conditional statement. My experiences as a performer, music director and educator have honed my problem-solving skills as well as my ability to work with many different people as a team.
Background

Most recently I served as the Director of Music Ministries at the United Methodist Church in Stow and Adjunct Instructor of Bassoon at the College of Wooster. I also teach bassoon privately, substitute in the Stow schools and am the Principal Bassoonist in the Lakeside Symphony Orchestra.

I have touched upon programming at several points my career, the very first time being in course work for a MA in Music Education. I used ASCII to manipulate sound envelopes and then use them in an arrangement of a short piece of music. My interest in coding was further piqued in more recent classes I took to renew my teaching licensure. I love the challenges and creative potential in coding.

I have a wonderful husband, two great kids and a spastic dog.
